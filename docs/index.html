Fire Departments Calculators

Below are three calculators used to answer common questions in and around fire fighting. Use the Info Button on each form for more details.

Equiptment Cost

This calculator determines the cost of basic fire Fighter equiptment. PPE and Radio Costs on Floating Numbers. Type and Pager are set based on selection.

Fire Flow Rates

Fire flow determines the amout of water a given fire requires to maintain control. Two formulas are used to calulate these values, NFA Academy, and Iowa University.

Water Supply

This calculator determines how long a particular Engine and Tender combination can pump water on a fire without being resupplied.
